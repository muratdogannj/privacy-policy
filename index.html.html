This Privacy Policy explains how Bubble Survivor ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use the game. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Bubble Survivor does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number, etc.).

The only data processed is anonymous, aggregated usage data collected automatically by third-party services described below.

2. Third-Party Services

The app displays ads powered by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant advertisements. This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policies.

  • Google Privacy Policy

3. Children's Privacy

Bubble Survivor is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can delete it.

4. Data Retention

We do not store any user data on our servers. Game progress (high score, unlocked upgrades) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us.
